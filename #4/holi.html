Holi





Holi is a religious spring festival celebrated by Hindus, as a festival of colours. It is primarily observed in India and Nepal. It is observed by the minority Hindus in Bangladesh and Pakistan as well in countries with large Indic diaspora populations following Hinduism, such as Suriname, Malaysia, Guyana, South Africa, Trinidad and Tobago, the United Kingdom, the United States, Mauritius, and Fiji. It is also known as Phagwah and Festival of Colours, or as Doajatra in Odisha and Dol Jatra or Basantotsav in West Bengal and Assam..
